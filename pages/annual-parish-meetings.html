Annual Parish Meetings

The Annual Parish Meeting (APM) is held in April. This meeting gives residents an opportunity to meet their Councillors and to raise anything of concern or interest in a more informal setting.

The Chairman presents an Annual Report and the Annual Accounts are available. One of our Ward Councillors will normally attend the APM.
